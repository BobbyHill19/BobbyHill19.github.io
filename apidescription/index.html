FatSecret Platform API

Parent Company: FatSecret

Data/Content:
  • Summary of food, exercise, and weight activity.
  • Food and nutrition search and detailed results.
  • A food diary - for planning and tracking foods eaten
  • An activity diary - to estimate and record energy utilization for various exercises
  • A weight chart and journal - to set goals and track progress
 Possible Uses: Creating an application that allows users to find company products that will be beneficial to their personal fitness goals.
The company can then measure user data to help increase sells of products.
